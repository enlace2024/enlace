About Us

Enlace,a platform designed for connecting alumni and students serves as an online community
that fosters engagement, collaboration, and networking between current students and graduates of an
institution. This platform aims to create a dynamic space where alumni can give back to their alma mater
by offering guidance, mentorship, job opportunities, and industry insights, while students can
benefit from valuable advice, internships, career opportunities, and networking.
